Overview

Contact Dr. Simon Cockell at the Bioinformatics Support Unit for details on obtaining access to the IPA system or having analyses carried out.

QIAGEN’S Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) has been broadly adopted by the life science research community and is cited in thousands of peer-reviewed journal articles. IPA helps you understand complex ‘omics data at multiple levels by integrating data from a variety of experimental platforms and providing insight into the molecular and chemical interactions, cellular phenotypes, and disease processes of your system. Powerful features in IPA, Causal Network Analysis, BioProfiler and My Findings will help you understand causal connections between diseases, genes networks of upstream regulators and leverage your own domain expertise and proprietary curation efforts.

Features displayed above include Pathway View, Networking / molecular interaction, Pathway Significance and Heat Tiles to disease functions.
