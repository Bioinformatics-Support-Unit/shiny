Differential Expression

Exiqon miRNA Microarrays - Array 1 of 2

Overview

Three comparisons were carried out for the first array : Control - Lymphoma | Control - High | High - Lymphoma. This section shows the summary of Differential Expression (DE) Analysis.

Below is a "Shiny" Application which will carry out real time adjustments to your plot and gene lists as you make changes. There are four tabs at the top: config, Volcano Plot, Filtered and Unfiltered. Config gives you the option to change your Contrast that you're investigating, the Adjusted P-Value it's filtered by and the Fold Change that it's filtered by. The Volcano plot tab, once selected, will show you where your significant miRNAs lie in comparison to others. Orange points denote significant by your Adjusted P-Value and Fold Change filters and blue if it's not significant. The points on the volcano plot bring up a tooltip on mouseover to show you the gene (or miRNA) name, Adjusted P-Value and log2 fold change. The Filtered and unfiltered tabs are the respective gene lists based on the config tab.

Any questions or feedback, let me know. - You can also filter your gene lists to look for miRNAs you're interested in. like hsa-miR-21, but to avoid the filter grabbing anything with hsa-miR-21 in the name, add a dollar sign to the end to signify that it's the end of the line in the query - hsa-miR-21$.
